# Decision 0005 — No Full Lockdown Claim

## Status

Accepted.

## Decision

The project will not claim complete Android device lockdown on normal consumer devices.

## Reason

A launcher can simplify the home experience, but Android system control varies by device, OEM, OS version, and management mode.

## Consequences

- No full-lockdown marketing.
- No enterprise MDM positioning.
- No kiosk claim in baseline.
- No notification-shade blocking claim.
- Device-owner/dedicated-device behavior, if ever considered, must be a separate future track.
